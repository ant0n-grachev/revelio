Revelio Privacy Policy

Revelio is a security-focused Chrome extension that helps detect potentially hidden text on webpages.

Summary

  • Revelio scans only the webpage you are currently viewing (the active tab), and only when you start a scan.
  • All processing happens locally in your browser.
  • Revelio does not send scanned content to any external server.
  • Revelio does not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

Data Revelio Accesses

Revelio accesses webpage content on the active tab only when you explicitly initiate a scan (for example, by clicking the extension button or a scan action in the extension UI).

This may include page text, styling information (such as colors and font sizes), and elements that could be used to hide text (for example, off-screen positioning or very small font sizes).

How Revelio Uses Data

The scanned page content is processed locally on your device to generate:

  • Scan results (a structured report of detected patterns)
  • Visual highlights on the page (to help you locate flagged content)

Data Storage

Revelio does not store your scanned page content on external servers. Any temporary data used to compute results exists only in memory while the scan is running.

Clipboard Use

Revelio copies results to your clipboard only when you click a button such as Copy JSON. Revelio does not access or read your clipboard contents.

Data Sharing

Revelio does not sell, transfer, or share user data with third parties. Revelio does not transmit scanned page content to external servers.

What Revelio Does Not Do

  • No advertising or marketing tracking
  • No analytics that collect browsing content
  • No profiling or building user identities
  • No use of data for creditworthiness, lending, or eligibility decisions

Third-Party Services

Revelio does not integrate with third-party analytics, ad networks, or external content-processing services.

Children’s Privacy

Revelio is not designed to collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on the same page with an updated “Last updated” date.
